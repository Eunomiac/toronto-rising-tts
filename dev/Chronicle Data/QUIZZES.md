## [TR-Q-033] What is the Vinculum?

### ANSWER: A — Pack loyalty bond
> The Vinculum is the emotional and supernatural glue inside a Sabbat pack. It forms through the Vaulderie. In the rite, pack members contribute vitae to a shared vessel, then drink, mixing blood from the group into each vampire. This breaks or weakens old Blood Bonds and replaces them with a new loyalty structure tied to the pack. That loyalty expresses as the Vinculum. It is not a single on or off bond. Each member develops a ranked pull toward the others, often uneven and sometimes unwanted. One vampire might feel fierce devotion to the ductus, irritation toward another packmate, and protective instinct toward a third. Those rankings shape pack politics, discipline, and violence. Packs exploit the Vinculum to survive fear, hunger, and internal betrayal. The same force also fuels obsession, jealousy, and punitive corrections when someone threatens cohesion.
* SUBJECTS: Sabbat, Vinculum, Vaulderie
