Multiclient Session Script (TOR-249 / TOR-144)

Goal: ~25–35 minutes. One linear script. Pass = no duplicate world effects, correct auto-seat, phase connect blindfold matches policy, join client stays connected.

Roles

Who Seat Drives
You (Host) Black / Storyteller (Steam auto-assign) All ST steps
Friend Chronicle PC color from C.PlayerData (auto-assign) Watch, report, one Roll, optional console line

Friend does not need the ST panel and should not manually pick a seat unless auto-assign fails.

Chronicle seat map: Thaumaterge→Brown, Hastur→Orange, PixelPuzzler→Red, JRook→Pink, Roarshack→Purple (lib/constants.ttslua).

Before the call

# Who Action
1 Host npm run build if UI changed; Save & Play on the Storyteller machine. Confirm you land on Black.

Phase A — Connect, auto-seat & authority (~5 min)

Friend Briefing

Phase A - Connection, Auto-Seat Assignment, Blindfolds, UI Visibility
You'll join my TTS game for ~25–30 minutes. Your seat color should assign automatically from your Steam account — tell me which color you land on (expect \). Watch for things moving twice, weird double sound fades, a stuck blindfold, or errors in chat. If I ask you to run a short console line and the number differs from mine but the table looks the same, that's a known quirk — just tell me both numbers. I'll ask you to click Roll once on your seat. You don't need to learn the mod — just tell me if something looks wrong compared to my screen.

Phase C — PC roll from join client (~5 min)

Friend Briefing

Phase C - Dice Roll System
Now we'll be making a few different rolls; I'll take this opportunity to walk you through how the dice system works. Be on the lookout for HUD elements that you don't think you're meant to see, anything that looks out of place, or anything that seems to be doing something you don't expect.

# Host Friend Pass if
C1
  • Click on your normal (black) dice token, above your character sheet.
  • Click several times on the same token to spawn additional dice into the pool
  • Note how many dice are in your pool, then click your Blood Surge bag to trigger that. Ensure a Rouse Die and your Blood Surge bonus were added to the pool.
  • Click the Rouse token to add a Rouse check to the pool. Click it again, confirm three Rouse dice are in the pool, separated into two groups.
  • Right-click the Blood Surge bag, confirm the Blood Surge has been reversed.
  • Left-click "ROLL" to roll manually, or right-click for automatic roll.
  • Click Spend Willpower. Check your sheet, confirm Willpower has been spent. Reroll three dice, confirm autoprogression of roll to next step.
  • Roll UI visible for friend
  • No duplicate spawns, smooth drawer movement.

If C2 double dice: file bug against roll / dice_bag routing.
