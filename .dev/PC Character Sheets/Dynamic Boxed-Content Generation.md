# Dynamic Boxed-Content Generation

Numerous elements on each character's sheet can be formatted as a rectangular panel containing some combination of child elements, such as headers, subheadings, dot-lines, text, and images. This document summarizes the different varieties of boxed content that can be generated, and summarizes the specifics of how to format them and what they include.
